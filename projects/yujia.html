Yujia data bus and message bus platform

Updated: July 2021

Java & SpringBoot & Mybatis MongoDB Kafka & RabbitMQ docker datawarehouse

Proejct Overview

The Yujia Message Bus serves as a bridge connecting different Kafka clusters. By configuring various Kafka connectors, it extracts data from databases or message middleware data sources, achieving data message aggregation. Additionally, its drag-and-drop interface facilitates ease of use for developers. The platform supports real-time stream computing, making it a comprehensive cross-platform product. Utilizing a technology stack that includes Kafka, RabbitMQ, RocketMQ, Redis, Spring Boot, React, and more.

Contributions

As a full-stack engineer, I played a significant role in the prototype design, feature development, debugging, and deployment of this product.

My contributions included creating functionalities to configure, start, stop, restart, and modify configurations of Kafka connectors via API, as well as logging their histories. Additionally, I routinely checked the health of Kafka connectors, restarting any that were malfunctioning.

In cases where a restart failed, I implemented SMS alerts as a fallback. Furthermore, by leveraging Kafka Streams, I performed filtering and cleansing of data sources to ensure data integrity and relevance etc.
